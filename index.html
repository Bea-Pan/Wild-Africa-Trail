Wild Africa Trail Experience

Travel Like No Other

We offer a unique opportunity for small groups to join our team of scientists on their projects in most beautiful and remote African locations. You will learn about their research methods and outcomes, you will get a chance to assist in their day-to-day projects and you will join them in their mission to protect the wild animals while supporting the local communities.

Our Mission

Bringing the opportunity to observe the wild life in a sustainable manner, while learning from specialists in the field.

Join our Legacy

We are passionate about the Wild Life and Environmental Protection and we want to share that passion with you!

The participation fee for our open-field labs is used to fund the research activities and protect the habitats.
